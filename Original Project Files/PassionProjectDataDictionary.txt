Passion Project Data Dictionary

Description of all features that I want to keep within the dataset

Gls: # of goals
Ast: # of assisted goals
xG: expected goals
Tkl: # of tackles
Nation: National team represented
Team: Club
Comp: One of Top 5 Leagues (Premier League, Ligue 1, Bundesliga, Serie A, La Liga)
MP: Matches played
Min: # of minutes played
90s: # of full 90 minute games completed
Starts: # of matches started
Subs: # of times coming off the bench as a substitute
Cmp: Completed Passes
Att: passes attempted
KP: # of key passes (passes that lead to goal scoring chances)
1/3: # of passes in opposing third of the field
npxG: Non-penalty expected goals
xAG: Expected Assisted goals
PrgP: Progressive Passes
Pos: Position on the field
