** Goalkeeping Data Dictionary **
-------------------------------
Player: Player name
Season: Season
Age: Current age
Nation: National Team
Competition: Top 5 League (Premier League, Ligue 1, Bundesliga, Serie A, La Liga)
MP: Matches Played
Min: Total Minutes Across the Season
90s: # of full 90 min games
Starts: # of times in starting lineup
Subs: # of substitutions
PSxG: Post Shot Expected Goals (how likely the keeper is to save the shot)
PSxG / SoT: Post Shot Expected Goals per Shot on Target
PSxG +/-: Post Shot Expected Goals Minus Goals Allowed
Opp: # of opponent attempted crosses
Stp: # of crosses stopped by keeper
Stp%: % of stopped crosses
#OPA: # of defensive actions outside of penalty area
AvgDist: Average distance from goal of all defensive actions
Att: Goal Kicks Attempted
Launch%: Percentage of goal kicks that were launched
AvgLen: Average Length of goal kicks (in yards)
GA: Goals Against
SoTA: Shots on Target Against
Saves: # of saves
Save%: Save Percentage
W: Wins
D: Draws
L: Losses
CS: # of clean sheets
CS%: clean sheet percentage
PKSave%: Penalty kick save percentage
