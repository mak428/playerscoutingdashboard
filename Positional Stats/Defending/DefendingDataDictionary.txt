** Defending Data Dictionary **
-------------------------------
Player: Player name
Season: Season
Age: Current age
Nation: National Team
Competition: Top 5 League (Premier League, Ligue 1, Bundesliga, Serie A, La Liga)
MP: Matches Played
Min: Total Minutes Across the Season
90s: # of full 90 min games
Starts: # of times in starting lineup
Subs: # of substitutions
Carries: # of times the player controlled the ball with their feet
TotDist: Total distance, in yards, a player moved the ball while controlling it with their feet, in any direction
PrgDist: Total distance player moved ball while controlling it with their feet towards the opponent's goal
Short Cmp: # of short passes completed
Short Att: # of short passes attempted
Short Cmp%: % of short passes completed
Medium Cmp: # of medium passes completed
Medium Att: # of medium passes attempted
Medium Cmp%: % of medium passes completed
Long Cmp: # of long passes completed
Long Att: # of long passes attempted
Long Cmp%: % of long passes completed
PrgC: Carries that move the ball towards the opponent's goal line at least 10 yards from its furthest point in the last six passes, or any carry into the penalty area. Excludes carries which end in the defending 50% of the pitch
1/3_C: Carries that enter final third
CPA: Carries into 18 yard box
Mis: # of failed ball controls
Dis: # of times player loses control of the ball after being tackled by opposing player
Rec: # of times player successfully received a pass
PrgR: Progressive Passes received
Won: Headers won
Lost: Headers lost
Won%: % of headers won
Tkl: # of tackles
TklW: # of tackles won
Def 3rd: Tackles in defensive third
Mid 3rd: Tackles in middle third
Att 3rd: Tackles in final (attacking) third
Challenges: # of challenges of dribblers
Challenges Att: # of dribbler challenges attempted
Challenge %: % of successful challenges attempted
Challenges Lost: # of challenges lost
Blocks: # of Blocks
Sh: # of shots blocked
Pass: # of passes blocked
Tkl+Int: # of tackles and interceptions
Clr: # of clearances
Err: # of errors leading to shots
